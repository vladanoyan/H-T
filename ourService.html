What is the VoIP telephony?

The VoIP telephony (Voice-over-IP) carries phone calls over an IP data network, whether on the internet or your own internal network.

What are the advantages of VoIP telephony?

The main advantage of using VoIP is the saving of cost calls since calls pass directly via the IP network and not through the network of a phone provider.

> You can work remotely, a simple internet connection is all you need.

> You profit from a secure tool for voice and data communication

> You can check the availability of your employees and see how to contact them

- Voice mail (direct accsess from the computer)
